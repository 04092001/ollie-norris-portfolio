Oliver Norris | Illustrator & Printmaker

The Tenth Heaven

"The Tenth Heaven" is a large etching that details the story of the apocryphal text, II Enoch. This plate depicts Enoch standing at the foot of the Tenth Heaven, where resides the great throne of God.

The piece tries to establish a new visual canon in regards to religious imagery, forefronting the otherworldliness and terror of holding communion with an ancient omnipotent being.
