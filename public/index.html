Oliver Norris | Illustrator & Printmaker

About Me

Ollie Norris is an illustrator and printmaker from Lancashire, working in London. He blends the rural ancient magic of Northern England with the grand architecture and history of London to create intricate and fantastical depictions of nature, occult and folk religion, and otherworldly beings. His work consists of delicate traditional fineline ink drawings, alongside prints, predominantly etchings.

The use of traditional methods is important in Ollie's work and these hand drawn images and painstakingly crafted prints reflect a more human side of art that displays the intricacies of the processes he employs.
