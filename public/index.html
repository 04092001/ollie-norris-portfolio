About Me

Oliver John Norris is a third year Illustration undergraduate living and studying in South East London. Originally from Rochdale in Greater Manchester, he developed a passion for print-making, etching, and a wide array of disciplines. In his spare time, he writes and plays music and is the lead bass for psychadelic indie rock band Fode.
